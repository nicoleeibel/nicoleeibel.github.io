To found a virtual company was a semester project at FH Joanneum. This project was done in collaboration with three university colleagues - Greta Grabner, Melanie Kraxner and Lisa Schelch.
We developed the corporate personality - found a company name and a slogan, created a logo and a realistic business plan. Furthermore we created a variety of designed material like visiting cards, stationery, information folder, carrier bags and give-aways (pack of cards, muffins, buttons).
