Hello!

My name is Nicole, I am a creative designer and fronted developer living in the southeast of Austria.

I studied information design at FH Joanneum Graz, University of Applied Sciences and graduated with a bachelor's degree this year. In 2010 I finished the college (higher education institute) for media and communication design of two years' duration.

Fields my studies covers are, among others, graphic design, web development, photography and video art as well as usability testing and user interface design.

Through my wide-ranging studies I enjoy being a generalist. Due to my skills in several fields and my interest in web development, I close the gap between designers and programmers.

Currently I am living in Stang, which is a small and lovely village between meadows and forests. Beside my passion for realizing my ideas I enjoy traveling and appreciate well tasting food. I can inspire me for almost everything and I love to learn something new.

I’m currently out of work, so if you are looking for someone like me or if you just like to get in contact, feel free to send me an email.
