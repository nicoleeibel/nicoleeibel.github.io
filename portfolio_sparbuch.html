Das Sparbuch

Publication

„Das Sparbuch“ is a creative field study based on the food-industry. The book has been designed by students (including me) at the FH Joanneum. The aim was to analyse elements and criteria of products and brands in order to create new artistic and communi­cative ways for conducting a field study. It became a book, that brings intellectual earnings, as well for the creators as for the viewers.
